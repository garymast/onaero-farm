Locally Grown Organic Produce

We are a generational family owned business, based in Onaero, 25km east of New Plymouth. Our produce is spray free and only sold in season, straight from the tree to you. No chillers or chemical ripening. Fruit how nature intended.

Select produce is available at different times thoughout the year. Our standard offerings are Avocados, Oranges, Lemons, Macadamias, Potatoes, Sweet Potatoes, Feijoas, Passionfruit, Honey and Eggs. Keep an eye on our socials and Subscribe Below to keep updated on what's currently in season.
